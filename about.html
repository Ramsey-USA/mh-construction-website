About MH Construction

Building with honesty, integrity, and trust for over a decade

Your Trusted General Contractor

MH Construction is a veteran-owned general contracting company committed to delivering exceptional construction services across Washington, Oregon, and Idaho.

With over 150 years of combined commercial construction experience, our team brings unparalleled expertise to every project. We specialize in commercial, medical, religious, winery, industrial, and tenant improvement construction.

Our approach is built on small-town values of honesty, transparency, and accountability. We believe in clear communication, transparent pricing, and delivering on our promises every time.
